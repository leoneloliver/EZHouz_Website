EZHouz API

Group of methods that supports EZHouz app through info and statistics about zones, districts, and affordable housing in Toronto.

These material is extracted from Google API and city's portal, via open data.

[ base url: /api/v1 , api version: 1.0.0 ]

Contact us

Bedroom Configurations

Simple types of bedroom configuration in an affordable housing.

/bedroomconfigs
List of bedroom configurations

response:

[{"Room":" 1"}, {"Room":" 2"}, {"Room":" B"}, {"Room":"1"}, ...]

Districts

Information about districts, according to this official map.

/districts
List of districts

response:

[{"Id":1, "Name":"Etobicoke York", "Color":"Blue"}, ...]

/districts/{id}
Details of a district

response:

{"Id":1, "Name":"Etobicoke York", "Color":"Blue", "Neighborhoods":[{"Id":20, "Name":"Alderwood", "Population":11900, "ViolencePercentage":0.384474546}, ...]}

Housing

Affordable housing that belongs to social programs.

/housing
List of housing

response:

[{"Id":1, "Bedrooms":"1,2,3,1W,2W", "Name":"FOREST MANOR II", "Address":"20 HUMBERLINE DRIVE", "Provider":"PNP", "Image":"IMAGE URL", "OverallRating":1}, ...]

/housing/{id}
Details of a housing

response:

{"Id":514, "Bedrooms":"1,2,3,4,2W", "Name":"WILCOX CREEK HOUSING CO-OPERATIVE INC", "Address":"22 FOREST CREEK PATHWAY", "Provider":"CO-OP", "Image":"IMAGE URL", "MaintenanceRating":10, "PestInfectionRating":0, "ZoneRatings":[{"Id":0, "Name":"Violence", "Value":2},...], "OverallRating":7, "Reviews":9, "Neighborhood":"Rouge", "Latitude":"43.8184267", "Longitude":"-79.21196189999999"}}

Zones

Regions delimited in housing map, according to this document.

/zones
List of zones

response:

[{"Id":1, "Name":"ZONE1"},...]

/zones/{id}
Details of a zone

response:
